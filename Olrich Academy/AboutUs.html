Welcome to Olrich Home of Technology Training Solutions

We thank you for choosing us as your doorway to Information and Communication Technology world and strongly believe that we will assist you in closing your digital gaps. Olrich Home of Technology Training Solution is a division of Olrich home of Technology a company started in 2005 with a wealth experience in ICT. We are registered, accredited and recognized Information Communication and Technology training provider. We offer programmes that will prepare you to face the challenges and realities of the 21st Century. Our accreditation number is ACC 2014/09/3144 for MICTSETA authorizing us to offer Further Education and Training National Certificates from Level 3 to Level 4 as well as ZA170624 for International Computer Driving Licence (ICDL) Programmes.

We start with pogrammes that will equip you with end user computing skills out and out, to development of applications as well as technical support or IT Technician through student oriented training offered by highly qualified lecturers.
